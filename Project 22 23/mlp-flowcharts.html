Multi-lateral funds flowcharts

The first flow chart show the flow of funds from implementing agencies to countries and MLFs. The colours represent the different implementing agencies (UNDP, ADB, AFDB etc).

The second flow chart shows the flow of funds from the MLFs (adapt ation funds) to the countries and to the implementing agencies. So, this shows that the majority of the LDCF is disbursed by and implemented by the UNDP. Similarly, a large proportion of the PPCR is disbursed and implemented by the World Bank.
