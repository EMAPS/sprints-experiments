Multi-lateral funded projects country profiling per categories

This visualisation shows the kinds of projects - in terms of sector â€“ for which funds have been committed through the various MLFs. Using the same categorisations as the NAPA projects database* we identified the kinds of projects funded through the various MLFs. These funds are the: Adaptation Fund (AF), the Least Developed Countries Fund (LDCF), the Special Climate Change Fund (SCCF) and the Pilot Programme for Climate Resilience (PPCR).

Since the amount requested varied significantly from country to country, we divided the countries into three groups: those that requested less that $10M; those that requested more than $10M but less than $100M; and those that requested more than $100M. We did this to make it easier to read the results.

* NAPA categories = cross-sectoral; food security; coastal zones/marine ecosystems; early warning systems and disaster management; education/capacity building; energy; health; infrastructure; insurance; terrestrial ecosystems; tourism; water resources.
